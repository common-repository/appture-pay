Unify Payment and Shipping Providers with Appture Pay, for merchants in South Africa.

== Description ==
Unify Payment and Shipping Providers with Appture Pay to allow event driven pay-outs to your service providers. Appture Pay adds a Payment Provider and a Shipping Provider to your Woocommerce installation. Just create a quick Appture Pay account, add a Client and your on your way to receiving payments as well as dispatching a courier in order to fulfill your orders. For merchants residing in South Africa.

== Installation ==
1. Head over to the Appture Pay website and complete the following steps
a. Create an account at https://www.appturepay.com
b. Setup your Company Profile
c. Add a confidential Client for your Woocommerce website
2. Add your Client ID and Client Secret to our Plugin\'s Settings page
3. Set the Redirect and Callback URL's as indicated on our Plugin\'s Settings page
